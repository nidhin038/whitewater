How to integrate car rental system with your existing website or new template?

Install script in any sub folder. for example "car-rental" folder.

so your front-end url: http://www.yourwebsite.com/car-rental/

Next, create a body blank page from your existing website design or new template.

Now copy & paste bellow html code in blank body of your design:

<iframe  src="http://www.yourwebsite.com/car-rental/" frameborder="0" scrolling="auto" width="650" height="800" ></iframe>

Remember you must change src(front-end url of booking system). Height and Width must be changed as your fit.

How can manage car Inventory(car manager)?

First Login in your Admin Panel. You can see following Option under "Car Manager"

  • Car Features: Here you can insert all possible car features. i.e. 5 seater, 4 Door, Air Conditioning, CD Player etc.
  • Car Types: Here you can insert all possible car type/class. i.e. Compact, Intermediate, Subcompact etc.
  • Car Vendors: Here you can insert all possible car vendors/company. i.e. BMW, Ford, Hyundai etc.
  • Car List: Here you can add/edit all car with price/day. image etc.
  • Location Manager: here you can add car pickup-dropoff location list.

How can manage price?

At the time of car rent price set per day basis. price set for 24 hours. if any customer book car for 25 hours, that will cost 2 days price.

example: Pick Up & Drop-off: 27/07/2012 9AM & 28/07/2012 9AM = 1 days

                  Pick Up & Drop-off: 27/07/2012 9AM & 28/07/2012 10AM = 2 days (because rent time > 24 hours)

 

First Login in your Admin Panel. You can see following Option under "Price Manager"

  • Prepaid Amount Setting: You can set prepaid amount(%) based on duration of rent. that can be unlimited combination.
  • Discount upon Duration: You can set discount amount(%) based on duration of rent. that can be unlimited combination.
  • Car Extras/Options: You can set unlimited car extras or options set price/day. i.e. GPS Navigation, car driver etc.
  • Price Calculation Setup: you can setup three of price calculation. describe bellow in details.

 

Important Note for price setup(Must read):

You Must define price of Daily and Hourly in car add/edit.

How different type of price setup work?

Example:
Pick-up Date & Time: 21/08/2012 9:00 AM
Drop-off Date & Time: 23/08/2012 3:00 PM

Daily: Price will be calculated of 3 days and that apply daily price.

Hourly: Price will be calculated of 54 hours and that apply hourly price.

Daily & Hourly Combined: Price will be calculated of 2 Day(s) 6 Hour(s) and that apply 2 days daily price + 6 hours hourly price.
